About Us

Some important information about D.A.V. School (Bhabua).

D.A.V. School Bhabua (Kaimur) was established in 2012 and since then it has been training the flame of D.A.V. excellence and creating new breakthrough in the world of education.This school is managed by Maa Mundeshwari Educational Trust, Bhabua (Kaimur) under the guidance of Bihar Rajya Arya Pratinidhi Sabha, Patna (Bihar).

This DAV school derives the ideas from the members of the Pratinidhi Sabha and their associates to improve and develop the school. It is an English medium, Co-educational institution running under the experienced management and qualified & experienced teachers. Individual attention is our speciality. We are conducting classes from Play Group to XII standard.

What we do

Our Vision & Visonaries

The D.A.V institutions are the gift of the Arya Samaj, a progressive movement founded by Swami Dayanand Saraswati in 1875. Swami Dayanand Saraswati, the great builder of modern India, Led a crusade against the superstitious and pernicious society that prevailed in contemporary India.

With a view to bring about a balanced and harmonious development of the future citizens of the country and to infuse in them ideas of Vedic Culture and high sense of patriotism, Arya Samaj founded a school at Lahore in 1886 by the name of 'Dayanand Anglo Vedic School'.

Our Aims & Objectives

Our mission at DAV School is to promote high academic expectations, and create an environment where the school community grows into peace building disciples and productive citizens.

DAV School is proud to offer a strong academic programme along with a complete offering of extracurricular programmes to suit the various needs and interests of our students as arts, science and technology and develop their total personality on progressive lines with a comprehensive mental outlook and to develop self confidence based on sound character so that they may lead successful life.
